Determines the mode- and band configurations used by the users input. Returns either one or two lists depending on the input.

Parameters:

  • mode (str, optional) – The mode in which the reduction is to be executed. Either ‘coherent’, ‘incoherent’ or ‘both’.

  • band (str, optional) – The band in which the reduction is to be executed. Either ‘lband’, ‘nband’ or ‘both’.

Returns:

  • modes (list of str) – A list of the modes to be enhanced.

  • bands (list of str) – A list of the bands to be enhanced.
